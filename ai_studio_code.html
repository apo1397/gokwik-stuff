About Klotho

Klotho Rules allow you to tailor risk detection to mark a customer as High Risk based on your specific business rules and needs. By defining these custom parameters, you can ensure your risk management strategy is perfectly aligned with your operational requirements.

Pro Tips
  • Maintain a high cut-off to avoid false positives.
  • Velocity helps prevent bot attacks.
  • Cart Value is recommended to be 4x your AOV. In case AOV is increasing then this value needs to be updated.
  • Place test orders using blocked keywords
